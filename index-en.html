The way Onam came ...

Frightened by our rule, the deities approached Lord Vishnu. Lord Vishnu, who was giving me presents at the victory celebrations of our country, approached me in the guise of a dwarf and asked for three feet of land. When the demand was accepted, Lord Vishnu transformed into the form of Trivikrama and measured heaven on the first foot and earth on the second. Noam gave me our heads to keep our word and Lord Vishnu humbled me to hell. But it also gave me the opportunity to visit our beloved people once a year. It is this vision of ours that the people of Kerala celebrate as Onam.
